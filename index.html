About Our Team

We're a small but passionate group of developers focused on creating tools that enhance online communities and developer experiences.

Orion Dev was founded by three developers who believe in the power of community-driven development. While we're just starting out with zero public projects yet, we're actively working behind the scenes on Discord bots, API tools, and utilities that solve real problems for developers and community managers.

Our philosophy is simple: build useful tools, share our knowledge, and grow together with the open-source community. We're currently in the early stages, focusing on foundational projects that will form the backbone of our future offerings.

How to get your Discord Widget:
  1. Go to your Discord Server Settings → Widget
  2. Enable Server Widget
  3. Copy the HTML embed code
  4. Paste it in the box above

Your widget will look like: <iframe src="https://discord.com/widget?id=YOUR_SERVER_ID&theme=dark" ...></iframe>
